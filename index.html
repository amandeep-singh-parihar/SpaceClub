Space Club

At our space club, our activities are not limited to classroom training. We conduct many activities including space camps, Sky Safari, Space Quizzes and webinars. We want to create a culture of fearlessly thinking, designing, and building ideas in the field of space research. Just like space, we will never have any boundaries on thinking, which will lead us toward the great ideas. We will work honestly and morally to bring those ideas into the motion. We want to spread the power of optimism in the community where we serve and we will expand the community day by day.
